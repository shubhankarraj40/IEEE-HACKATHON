About the Hackathon

/* What is TechNova? */

TechNova is an intensive 48-hour hackathon where participants from different disciplines come together to solve real-world problems. Whether you're a programmer, designer, or entrepreneur, this event offers an opportunity to showcase your skills and collaborate with like-minded individuals.

This year's theme is "AI for Social Good" where we challenge participants to develop innovative solutions using artificial intelligence and machine learning to address societal challenges such as healthcare, education, environmental sustainability, and accessibility.
